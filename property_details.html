Peerless details & a rich Hollywood history awaits in this English Country estate by architect, Gordon Kaufmann, located on a quiet tree-lined street in Hancock Park. Step into the stately formal entry which leads to the magnificent 3-story, wood paneled formal living room. Hand carved details embellish the fireplace, period moldings and doors. A den reminiscent of an English pub boasts a grand bar, hand hewn stonework & French doors that open to the gardens. Entertain in the gracious dining room, w/ direct access to the remodeled kitchen w/ Miele appliances. Upstairs, a dramatic library offers the ultimate place to relax or work, w/ a hidden door that opens to the master suite overlooking the gardens. Enjoy tranquil grounds w/ a lagoon style pool, jacuzzi, & paths that meander along streams & koi ponds. A storybook guest cottage & apartment & a multitude of seating areas complete the unique property. A serene oasis in the heart of LA, minutes from the finest leisure & entertainment.
